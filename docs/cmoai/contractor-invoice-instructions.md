# Contractor Monthly Invoice — Instruction Message

Send this to each independent contractor (GF for events, content creators, etc.) once.
Paste into WhatsApp, swap `[name]`, attach `contractor-invoice-template.md` (or a PDF of it).

---

Hey [name] 👋

Quick admin thing so I can keep paying you properly under the new company.

From now on, send me **one invoice at the end of each month** for the work you did that month. Takes 2 minutes.

It needs:
1. Your name + IC
2. The month (e.g. "June 2026")
3. What you did + the amount (RM)
4. Your bank name + account number

Make it out to:
**CMO CONSULTING SDN. BHD. (202601024007)**

I'm sending a template — just fill in the blanks, save as PDF, and send it to me **by the last day of the month**.

That's it. Keeps everything clean for accounting and makes sure you get paid on time, every month 🙏

---

## Why this matters (for your reference, not the contractor)
- Independent contractors invoicing the company = no EPF/SOCSO obligation (vs. employees). Keeps them flexible and your payroll clean.
- Every payment now has a matching invoice → tax-deductible, audit-ready, and ready for e-invoicing later.
- Pay them as a **Bill** in Bukku (`createBill` already wired in event-ops) so it lands in the books against the right expense account.
